## Typography Scale

### Headings
- **H1 (Page Title)**: 
  - Mobile: 1.875rem (30px)
  - Tablet: 2.25rem (36px)
  - Desktop: 3rem (48px)

- **H2 (Section Title)**: 
  - Mobile: 1.25rem (20px)
  - Desktop: 1.5rem (24px)

- **H3 (Card Title)**: 
  - Mobile: 1.125rem (18px)
  - Desktop: 1.25rem (20px)

### Body Text
- **Regular**: 0.875rem - 1rem (14px - 16px)
- **Small**: 0.75rem - 0.875rem (12px - 14px)

- **xs**: 0.25rem (4px)
- **sm**: 0.5rem (8px)
- **md**: 1rem (16px)
- **lg**: 1.5rem (24px)
